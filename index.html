Our Mission

Minimizing Failures. Saving Energy. Building a Sustainable Future.

Every undetected fault in a power generation asset wastes energy, accelerates degradation, and contributes to unnecessary carbon emissions. PredicX plugs into your plant's SCADA system and uses that live operational data to train AI models that predict failures before significant power loss occurs — protecting your assets and the planet.

SCADA-Driven AI Training

Your plant's own operational data — live from your SCADA system — is what trains the model. No generic baselines. The AI learns the unique digital fingerprint of your specific installation.

Sustainability at the Core

Preventing energy loss and extending asset life directly reduces carbon output. PredicX's predictive intelligence helps operators maximize clean energy yield while minimizing waste and premature replacements.

Zero-Disruption Integration

PredicX connects to your existing digital infrastructure without hardware changes. Our platform ingests live data streams and surfaces actionable risk intelligence within hours of going live.

WINDWATCH

Turbine Blade Fatigue Analysis

Fibre-optic strain sensing and vibration spectral analysis to detect leading-edge erosion, delamination, and root cracking in wind turbine blades before structural failure occurs.

Drivetrain Health

Gearbox & Bearing Intelligence

High-frequency vibration fingerprinting of main bearings, gearbox, and generator — correlating harmonics to specific fault modes with confidence-scored remaining useful life estimates.

Tower & Foundation

Structural Integrity Monitoring

SHM sensor networks on tower walls and monopile foundations, detecting fatigue crack growth in steel welds and concrete degradation in offshore installations.

NUCLEUS

Reactor Vessel Monitoring

Continuous probabilistic assessment of reactor pressure vessel embrittlement and weld integrity, integrating neutron flux history with ultrasonic in-service inspection data.

Primary Loop

Coolant System Health

Acoustic leak detection and vibration-based health monitoring for primary coolant pumps, steam generators, and pressurizers — all safety-grade qualified.

Balance of Plant

Turbine & Balance of Plant

Predictive maintenance for nuclear turbine generators, feedwater pumps, and heat exchangers — extending run lengths and supporting license renewal programs.

FLOWGUARD

Pipeline Corrosion Detection

Inline inspection data fusion with ML-based corrosion growth modeling — predicting wall thinning hot spots and recommending optimal intervention windows to prevent ruptures.

Rotating Equipment

Compressor & Pump Health

Continuous monitoring of centrifugal and reciprocating compressors, detecting valve wear, piston rod fatigue, and impeller erosion before unplanned shutdowns.

Process Safety

Heat Exchanger Fouling

Real-time heat transfer efficiency tracking combined with predictive fouling models — optimising cleaning schedules and preventing tube failures in HEX bundles.

HYDROSCAN

Water Treatment Plant AI

Predictive monitoring for membrane filtration systems, pump stations, and clarifiers — detecting biofouling, membrane integrity failures, and chemical dosing drift before compliance breaches.

Dam & Hydro

Hydroelectric Asset Health

Structural health monitoring for dam bodies, penstock pipelines, and Francis/Pelton turbines — integrating geotechnical sensors with probabilistic seepage and settlement models.

Water Quality

Contamination Early Warning

Multi-parameter water quality sensor arrays with AI-driven anomaly detection — identifying contamination events, sensor drift, and treatment process upsets in near real time.

How It Works

From SCADA Data to Actionable Intelligence

A rigorous digital pipeline that takes your plant's live operational data and transforms it into confident, prioritised failure predictions — no new hardware required.

1

SCADA Connection

PredicX connects directly to your plant's existing SCADA system — no new hardware, no disruption. Your live operational data begins streaming to our platform instantly.

2

Data Ingestion

Historical and real-time SCADA data flows into our cloud platform — encrypted, latency-optimised, and continuously updated as your plant operates.

3

AI Model Training

Your plant's unique digital signature is learned. A probabilistic model is calibrated to your specific asset type, operational history, and environmental conditions.

4

Risk Scoring

Every asset receives a continuously updated failure probability score. The system flags anomalies and builds a prioritised risk heatmap — updated in real time.

5

Actionable Intelligence

Operators receive ranked risk alerts with predicted degradation timelines, estimated energy loss, and recommended intervention windows — weeks before failure.
